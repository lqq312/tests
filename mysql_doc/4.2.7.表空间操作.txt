表空间操作

	+------------------------------------------------------------+----------+----------------+------------------------+------------------------+
	|                           Operation                        | In Place | Rebuilds Table | Permits Concurrent DML | Only Modifies Metadata |
	+------------------------------------------------------------+----------+----------------+------------------------+------------------------+
	| Enabling or disabling file-per-table tablespace encryption | No       | Yes            | No                     | No                     |
	+------------------------------------------------------------+----------+----------------+------------------------+------------------------+

启用/禁用每个表文件表空间加密：
	语法：
		ALTER TABLE tbl_name ENCRYPTION='Y', ALGORITHM=COPY;
